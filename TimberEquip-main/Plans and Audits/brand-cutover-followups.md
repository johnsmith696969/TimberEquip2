# Brand Cutover Follow-Ups

This checklist covers the remaining business-account and launch tasks for TimberEquip.

## Stripe

- Update the Stripe business name to `TimberEquip`.
- Update the public support email to `info@timberequip.com` when the inbox is live.
- Review the support phone number and business address shown on receipts, invoices, and the customer portal.
- Confirm statement descriptor, support URL, and branding settings use the TimberEquip name.
- Re-test a live or test checkout after the business-profile update.

## Twilio

- Verify `info@timberequip.com` inside Twilio once the mailbox is ready.
- Update the Twilio account profile, notification email, and any business identity fields to TimberEquip.
- Confirm the brand phone number is `612-600-8268` anywhere Twilio messaging, voice, or verification flows expose a contact number.
- Review webhook alert emails and admin notifications so they go to the right TimberEquip inboxes.

## SEO

- Confirm production canonical URLs point to `https://www.timberequip.com`.
- Confirm sitemap and robots files use the new domain.
- Verify Google Search Console and Google Business/Profile-related links for the new domain.
- Recheck structured data, Open Graph, and organization schema for TimberEquip branding.
- Make sure any remaining `timberequip.com` references are removed before final domain cutover.

## Redirects

- Create a dedicated `301 redirects` planning page before launch.
- Document every old `timberequip.com` URL that needs to redirect to the matching `timberequip.com` URL.
- Include homepage, category pages, listing pages, legal pages, blog URLs, and any ad landing pages.
- Add a validation step to test the redirects after go-live.
